But despite the distance, you continue to make me feel loved, valued, and special.

Thank you for being patient with me, for always making time for our conversations, and for loving me even when we can't be together physically. Every message, call, and "I love you" means so much to me. You have become one of the best parts of my life, and I am grateful for every moment we share.

I miss you more than words can express, but I am proud of us for staying strong and choosing each other every day. The distance may test us, but it also reminds me how much you mean to me and how worth it our love truly is.

As we celebrate another month together, I want you to know that no matter how far apart we are, you are always in my thoughts and in my heart. I look forward to the day when the distance is no longer between us and we can create beautiful memories side by side.

Thank you for being my happiness, my comfort, and my favorite person. I love you today, tomorrow, and every day after that. Happy Monthsary, my love. 💕

forever yours, Maila ♡
